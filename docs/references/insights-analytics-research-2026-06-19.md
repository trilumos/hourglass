# Insights / Analytics — Competitive Research (2026-06-19)

> Deep-research pass (fan-out search → fetch → adversarial verify → synthesize)
> on what focus / productivity / time-tracking / meditation / habit apps show in
> their stats/insights, free-vs-paid gating, and motivational framing — to inform
> **Sustain's enhanced Pro Insights**. Findings below are the verified claims.

## 1. The big patterns

**A single 0–100 "score" is established precedent (validates Focus Score).**
- **RescueTime "Productivity Pulse"** — a 0–100 composite, transparent formula
  weighting time across 5 productivity tiers. They *explain* the number.
- **Rize "Focus Quality Score"** — blends 20+ work attributes (deep vs shallow
  time, interruptions, context-switching) into a daily score + per-session
  "Workblocks." The richest analog to our Focus Score / Focus Stamina: a
  *quality* score, not just total time. Rize also gates **AI coaching that
  explains your focus** behind premium.
- **Opal** — focus score + streaks + distracting-vs-productive breakdown.
- Takeaway: our 0–100 Focus Score is on-trend. The opportunity is to **show its
  trend over time** and **explain how it's computed** (RescueTime's transparency).

**Free vs paid: the consistent paywall levers are depth, trends, export, history.**
- **Focus Keeper guide:** free = basic daily-session bar charts; **Pro unlocks
  heatmaps, peak-productivity windows, weekly trend summaries.**
- **RescueTime:** free caps history (14 days / 3 months) and gates the score,
  daily goals, focus features, advanced analytics (Premium ~$12/mo, $78/yr).
- **Toggl:** custom reports are paid (free sees titles, can't open).
- **Be Focused:** **CSV export + detailed reports = Pro** (basic reports free).
- **Forest:** custom tags Pro-gated (deep stats otherwise present).
- **Pomofocus:** project tracking + yearly reports = paid.
- Takeaway: **basic stats free; heatmaps, peak-time windows, trends, export,
  tag/project breakdowns, and history-depth are the things people pay for.** This
  validates Sustain's plan (free = Records + heatmap; Pro = the analytical depth).
  Note: many apps actually gate the *heatmap* too — keeping ours free is a
  deliberately generous, "value-upfront" move (see Insight Timer below).

**Counter-model — generous free tier wins retention.**
- **Insight Timer** keeps a large free tier + forgiving streak (counts partial
  sessions); reportedly **~16% Day-30 retention vs <8.5% for Calm/Headspace.**
  Value-upfront + low-friction progress beats aggressive paywalls. (Tension to
  respect: don't over-gate Insights.)

**Visualizations that are table-stakes:** calendar **heatmaps**;
daily/weekly/monthly/yearly **bar/line** breakdowns; **streaks** (current/best);
**time-of-day "peak window"**; **trends over time**; **period-over-period
comparison**; **tag/project donuts**; milestone badges.

**Motivational framing done right (for our honesty brand):**
- **Headspace "My Progress":** total minutes, current streak, sessions, avg
  duration + milestone **medals**; and validated scales (Perceived Stress Scale,
  GAD-7) — science-backed measures over vanity. (Honesty-aligned.)
- **Insight Timer milestones:** stars at 10 consecutive days + every 50
  cumulative days; "last 10 days / weeks / months" charts. A long-horizon ladder.
- **Finch:** insights "encouraging without being overwhelming"; themed Journeys
  (e.g. "building focus") that break a goal into small rewarded steps. Best model
  for *warm, non-preachy* copy.

## 2. Standout / differentiating ideas worth stealing
- **Peak-window *recommendation*** (Rize "Productivity Trends" pinpoints your
  high-focus windows) — turn our "when you focus" from a chart into advice:
  *"You focus best 9–11am."*
- **Explain the score** transparently (RescueTime) — a "how this is calculated"
  that builds trust (we already have a Focus Score page; extend the ethos).
- **Completion / follow-through rate** and **interruption/distraction frequency**
  (Rize, Focusflow) — *controllable* metrics, not vanity.
- **Focus-quality split** (deep vs shallow) — our analog is Flow vs Pomodoro/Custom
  and completed vs given-up.
- **Milestone ladder** (Insight Timer stars / Headspace medals) — long-horizon
  achievements; dovetails with our V2 Levels.
- **Personal-bests timeline** (Headspace-style records over time).

## 3. Anti-patterns to avoid (honesty rule)
- **Manufactured streak anxiety** — the litmus (Smashing Mag): *"Does your product
  make money by selling solutions to anxiety your product created?"* Avoid
  streak-loss warnings, "you'll lose your progress" alerts, streak-pets that
  "die," countdown guilt.
- **The streak paradox** (Trophy): 7-day-streak users return 2.4× / complete 3.6×
  — but if 90+ day-streak users retain *worse* than 30-day, the streak is creating
  unsustainable pressure. Match mechanics to real usage; offer a **streak freeze**
  / separate streak from the daily goal; frame as "how far you've come."
- **Vanity over actionable** — favor controllable-behavior metrics (Focus Stamina
  growth, completion rate, consistency) over impressive-but-empty totals; context
  + trend + segmentation are what make a number mean something.

## 4. Mapping → table-stakes / differentiating / avoid

| | Feature |
|---|---|
| **Table-stakes** (have or easy) | total focus, session count, streak (current/best), avg/longest session, daily/weekly/monthly breakdown, calendar heatmap, by-mode split, period comparison |
| **Differentiating** (lean in) | **Focus Score trend**, **Focus Stamina growth**, **peak-window recommendation**, **completion/follow-through rate**, "explain the score" transparency, personal-bests timeline, milestone ladder (→V2 Levels), CSV export |
| **Avoid** | streak-loss/guilt notifications, loss-aversion framing, vanity totals without context, fabricated benchmarks / fake "you're better than X%" |

## 5. Prioritized menu for Sustain's Pro Insights
Data we already record per session: **mode, planned vs actual duration, start
time, completion/give-up, intention text** (+ Focus Score, Focus Stamina).

| # | Idea | Uses existing data? |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Focus Score trend over time** (line) — the hero number's trajectory | ✅ yes |
| 2 | **Focus Stamina growth** (sustainable block length climbing) — *unique to Sustain* | ✅ yes |
| 3 | **Peak focus window** recommendation ("you focus best 9–11am") | ✅ yes (start times) |
| 4 | **Completion / follow-through rate** (+ trend) — controllable, honest | ✅ yes (completion flag) |
| 5 | **Personal-bests timeline** (records over time) | ✅ yes |
| 6 | **CSV / data export** (classic Pro lever) | ✅ yes |
| 7 | **Daily/weekly focus goal + progress** (ring + consistency) | ⚠ NEW: a goal setting |
| 8 | **"What you focused on"** (aggregate by intention) | ⚠ partial: intention is free-text → needs grouping/tags |
| 9 | **Milestone ladder** (long-horizon achievements) | → fits V2 Levels |

**Suggested free vs Pro line:** keep **Records + the consistency heatmap free**
(value-upfront, Insight-Timer lesson); make Pro = the current depth (range
toggle, focus-over-time, when-you-focus, by-mode donut, period comparison,
personalized copy) **plus #1–#6** above. #7 (goals) and #8 (intention tags) need
small new data capture — candidates for the build, not blockers.
